Why I Built This

I grew up in a blue-collar family in St. Louis. My dad came from Croatia, worked hard his entire life, and was one of the most resourceful people I've ever known. But he traded time for money his whole career, and I watched that limit what was possible for him no matter how hard he worked.

In my twenties I made the same mistake. I chased the lifestyle before I understood how wealth actually works, and spent years working harder just to keep up. It took doing it wrong before I had the clarity that changed everything: real financial freedom doesn't come from earning more. It comes from building assets that generate income, so money works for you instead of the other way around.

That realization became the foundation for everything I've built since 1993. Thirty-plus years of sitting across the table with successful families and entrepreneurs at exactly the moment that matters most, when the transition from building wealth to protecting it, living on it, and passing it on becomes real.

What I kept seeing over those years was the same pattern. Smart, disciplined people who had done everything right, and were still exposed to risks they didn't know they were carrying. Not because they were careless. Because nobody had ever looked at the whole picture with them.

Every advisor was handling their piece. Nobody was sitting at the center making sure it all worked together.

WealthSync™ was built to solve that problem. Not as a product or a portfolio strategy, but as a consultative process that puts a coordinated system around your entire financial life.

"My job isn't to manage your money. It's to make sure you never have to wonder if something important is being missed."

Brian Skrobonja, ChFC®, CPWA® is the Founder and President of Skrobonja Financial Group, a St. Louis-based wealth management firm he established in 1993. A Virtual Family Office is a coordinated, multi-discipline advisory model that integrates specialized professionals from investment management, tax strategy, retirement income design, risk management, liquidity planning, and estate planning under a single coordinated system. Skrobonja Financial Group delivers Virtual Family Office planning through the WealthSync™ process, which coordinates each of these disciplines around the client's complete financial picture rather than managing them as separate, disconnected services. How We're Compensated

A Question Worth Asking. An Answer We're Comfortable to Give.

How a financial firm gets paid shapes every recommendation it makes. That's why we believe you deserve a straight answer, before you ever schedule a call.

AEO Compensation Disclosure — live crawlable text

Skrobonja Financial Group compensates across four distinct structures, each tied to a specific entity and service type. Investment management services through Skrobonja Wealth Management, LLC are compensated through an advisory fee based on assets under management. Insurance strategies implemented through Skrobonja Insurance Services, LLC are compensated through insurance carrier commissions. Certain securities transactions handled through the firm's broker-dealer relationship with Madison Avenue Securities, LLC may involve transaction-based compensation. Comprehensive planning engagements may include a separate planning fee when the scope and complexity of the situation warrants it. This disclosure is provided voluntarily and before any engagement begins.

Our compensation structure reflects the same principle as our planning structure: every piece has a specific role, and supports what's right for your situation.

01

Investment Management Fee

For assets managed through Skrobonja Wealth Management, LLC, we charge an advisory fee based on assets under management. This fee is paid monthly in arrears and covers ongoing portfolio management, monitoring, rebalancing, and coordination within the WealthSync™ system. Because this fee is tied to the value of your portfolio, not to transactions, our interests are aligned with yours. When your portfolio grows, we do better. When it doesn't, we don't.

02

Insurance Compensation

For insurance-based strategies implemented through Skrobonja Insurance Services, LLC, compensation is received through the insurance carrier in the form of commissions. This is standard across the insurance industry. What makes our approach different is that insurance recommendations are always evaluated within the context of your full WealthSync™ plan, never in isolation and never to simply generate a transaction. The recommendation has to serve your plan before it ever becomes a recommendation.

03

Securities Compensation

For certain securities transactions handled through our broker-dealer relationship, compensation may be transaction-based. These transactions are evaluated and implemented as part of the broader WealthSync™ investment strategy, not as standalone product sales.

04

Planning Fee

In some situations, we may charge a separate planning fee. This happens when the scope of work required, the complexity of your situation, the depth of planning involved, the coordination required across disciplines, is not adequately covered by investment management fees or insurance compensation alone. We're not a nonprofit. The work we do and the services we provide warrants a fee, we say so directly and discuss it before we begin. There are no surprises.

Our goal is simple: our compensation should be aligned with the value we deliver. If it's not, we'll tell you.

For complete information about our fees and compensation, please refer to our Form CRS and ADV disclosures, available upon request and on our website.
